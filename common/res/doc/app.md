# Pages Documentation
This document provides an overview of the various pages available in the application, categorized by their functionality.

## General Pages
- **Blog Page**: Displays a list of blog posts and articles.
- **Blog Post Page**: A detailed view of a single blog post.
- **Home Page**: The landing page of Wondamart GH, providing an overview and navigation to other sections.
- **Contact Page**: A form for users to reach out with questions or feedback.
- **About Page**: Contains information about Wondamart GH, its purpose, and the team behind it.
- **Terms & Policies**: Outlines the terms of service and privacy policies for users about bundles and more.
- **FAQ Page**: A page with frequently asked questions and answers.
- **Pricing Page**: Displays pricing plans and subscription options of bundles.
- **Features Page**: Highlights the key features and benefits of Wondamart GH.
- **404 Page**: A custom error page for handling non-existent routes.

## Authentication Pages
- **LoginPage Page**: A secure page for users to log into their accounts.
- **Register Page**: A page for new users to create an account.
- **Password Reset Page**: Allows users to reset their passwords if forgotten.
- **Email Verification Page**: A page for users to verify their email addresses.
- **OTP Page**: A page for users to enter a one-time password for additional security.
- **Two-Factor Authentication Page**: A page for setting up and managing two-factor authentication.

## User Account Pages
- **Dashboard Page**: Displays user-specific data and analytics.
- **Profile Page**: Shows user profile information and allows for editing.
- **Settings Page**: Allows users to customize their preferences and account settings.
- **Notifications Page**: Displays user notifications and alerts.
- **History Page**: Shows the past transactions or activities of the user.
- **Cart Page**: Displays items added to the user's cart.

## Purchase Pages
- **Checkout Page**: A secure page for completing purchases.
- **Subscription Page**: Allows users to manage their subscriptions and billing information.
- **Order Confirmation Page**: A summary page displayed after a successful purchase.
- **Payment Methods Page**: A page for users to add, remove, or update their payment methods.
- **Invoice Page**: Displays a list of user invoice and billing for the purchase.
